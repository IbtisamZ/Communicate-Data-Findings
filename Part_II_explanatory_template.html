Investigation Overview¶

The goal with this presentation is to pinpoints major relationships and correlations of different factors impacting rides in the bike-sharing system. We've been able to get insights throughout our data analysis earlier that showed the majority of riders are of gender male. We also we able to use trip duration distribution and know how long a trip takes on average, which turned out to be 5-15 minutes. Users of type "Subscribers" were the majority of riders. And younger people had longer trip durations, and vice versa. It surely was interesting to see and find these major findings through our analysis.

Dataset Overview and Executive Summary¶

The chosen dataset is Ford GoBike System Data. The dataset includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area. The dataset consists of 180k+ rows and 16 columns.
